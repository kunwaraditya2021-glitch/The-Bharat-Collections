Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, shipping, and policies

About Our Products

What materials are used in The Bharat Collections? ‚ñ∂

We use premium quality materials including 100% organic cotton, cotton blends, linen, and other sustainable fabrics. All materials are carefully sourced to ensure comfort, durability, and environmental responsibility. Each product is crafted to provide the best balance of quality and affordability.

Are your products vegan and cruelty-free? ‚ñ∂

Most of our collections are vegan-friendly as we primarily use plant-based fabrics like cotton and linen. Our manufacturing partners follow ethical practices and maintain strict animal welfare standards. We do not test on animals and support cruelty-free production methods.

What's the difference between your product ranges? ‚ñ∂

Heritage Prints: Traditional Indian patterns with contemporary cuts. Classic Basics: Timeless essentials in premium quality. Summer Vibes: Lightweight, breathable collection for warm weather. Each range celebrates different aspects of Indian fashion while maintaining our quality standards.

Do you offer international shipping? ‚ñ∂

Currently, we ship across India (pan-India delivery). International shipping is coming soon. Subscribe to our newsletter to be notified when we expand globally. For now, we focus on serving the Indian market with quality products and excellent service.

How do I know my size? ‚ñ∂

Each product page includes a detailed size guide with measurements in centimeters. You can compare your measurements with our size chart to find the perfect fit. If you're between sizes, we recommend ordering the larger size for comfort. Our return policy makes it easy to exchange if needed.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does delivery take? ‚ñ∂

We deliver within 3-5 business days across India. Delivery time may vary based on your location and current order volume. You'll receive a tracking ID via email as soon as your order ships, allowing you to monitor delivery status in real-time.

Is shipping free? ‚ñ∂

Yes! We offer free shipping on all orders across India. There are no hidden charges or minimum order requirements. Our commitment is to provide affordable, quality fashion with transparent pricing.

Can I track my order? ‚ñ∂

Absolutely! Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can track your package through our partner Qikink's tracking system or on your account dashboard. We also send SMS updates for major milestones during delivery.

What areas do you deliver to? ‚ñ∂

We deliver to all addresses across India including metro cities, tier-2 cities, and even tier-3 towns. Whether you're in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, or a small town, we ensure your order reaches you safely. Our partnership with Qikink enables this extensive pan-India reach.

Can I change my delivery address after ordering? ‚ñ∂

Yes, you can change your delivery address within 24 hours of placing your order. Contact our customer support team immediately with your order number and the new address. After 24 hours, the order enters the shipping queue and cannot be modified.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy? ‚ñ∂

We offer a hassle-free 7-day return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 7 days of delivery. The product must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging. We'll process refunds within 5-7 business days after receiving the returned item.

How do I initiate a return? ‚ñ∂

Log into your account, select the order, and click "Return/Exchange." Follow the steps to generate a return label. Pack your item securely and drop it off at any Qikink pickup point. You can also call our support team for assistance with the return process.

Do I have to pay for return shipping? ‚ñ∂

For returns due to defects or our mistake, return shipping is completely free. For returns due to customer preference, we provide a prepaid return label, making the process convenient and transparent. You can choose to pay for return shipping and receive a full refund or exchange for free.

Can I exchange a product for a different size or color? ‚ñ∂

Yes! Exchanges are easy and free. Simply initiate the exchange request within 7 days of delivery, select the new size or color, and we'll ship it to you at no extra cost. Once we receive your return, the new item will be dispatched within 2-3 business days.

What if my product arrives damaged? ‚ñ∂

Contact our support team immediately with photos of the damaged product and packaging. We'll arrange a free replacement or full refund without requiring you to return the damaged item. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Payments & Orders

What payment methods do you accept? ‚ñ∂

We accept all major payment methods: Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), Debit Cards, UPI, Net Banking, Wallets (PayTM, PhonePe, Google Pay), and Cash on Delivery in select areas. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

Is my payment information safe? ‚ñ∂

Your security is our priority. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and comply with PCI DSS standards for payment processing. Your payment information is never stored on our servers and is processed through secure third-party gateways.

Can I place bulk orders? ‚ñ∂

Yes! We welcome bulk and wholesale orders. For orders above 50 units, please contact our B2B team at wholesale@bharatcollections.com for special pricing and customization options. We can offer competitive rates for businesses and organizations.

Can I cancel my order? ‚ñ∂

You can cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it before it enters the shipping queue. After 24 hours, the order is already being prepared for shipment. Contact our support team immediately for cancellation requests. Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days.

How do I apply a discount code? ‚ñ∂

During checkout, you'll see a field for "Promo Code" or "Discount Code." Enter your valid code and click "Apply." The discount will be reflected in your order total before payment. Check your email for exclusive codes or subscribe to our newsletter for special offers.

Account & General

How do I create an account? ‚ñ∂

Visit our website and click on "Sign Up" or "Create Account." Enter your email and create a password, or sign up using your Google or social media account. You'll receive a verification email to confirm your account. Having an account helps track orders and saves your preferences.

Can I shop without creating an account? ‚ñ∂

Yes! You can shop as a guest. Simply add items to your cart and proceed to checkout. You'll be able to enter your email for order confirmation without creating a formal account. However, having an account provides benefits like order history and faster checkout.

How do I reset my password? ‚ñ∂

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address and we'll send a password reset link. Click the link, create a new password, and you're done! If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.

How do I contact customer support? ‚ñ∂

You can reach us via email at support@bharatcollections.com or call +91 98765 43210 (10 AM - 6 PM IST). You can also use the contact form on our website. We respond to all queries within 24 hours. For urgent issues, please call our support hotline.

Do you offer a loyalty or rewards program? ‚ñ∂

Join our newsletter to get exclusive discounts on your first purchase and special member-only offers. Every purchase earns you points that can be redeemed for future discounts. Loyalty members also get early access to new collections and special sales.
